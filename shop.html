PoE

Power over Ethernet (or PoE) is a cost-effective way to provide power and data to a PoE-enabled device using a single cable, without needing to rely on nearby power outlets. TRENDnet offers a wide-range of PoE solutions, such as PoE switches, injectors, extenders, splitters, access points, hardware controllers, IP cameras, and NVRs. In addition to reduced cost and time, TRENDnet's PoE solutions have several other benefits, including flexibility for remote installations, network scalability, and managed controls.
